Creamy Pepper Jack Pasta

Yummy Creamy Pepper Jack Pasta Recipe

Pulled this one from the internet and it has become a family favorite

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Penne Pasta
  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1 1/2 Cups Heavy Cream
  • 1/3 cup Pepperoncini Juice
  • 1 Tablespoon Garlic Salt
  • Salt + Pepper To Taste
  • 1 1/2 Cups Freshly Grated Pepper Jack Cheese
  • 1 Cupe Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions, drain and set aside.
  2. Meanwhile add the butter and cream to a large skillet and melt the butter over medium low heat.
  3. Once the butter is melted, stir and simmer the mixture for 2 minutes.
  4. Slowly pour in the pepperoncini juice while whisking constantly.
  5. Add the garlic, red pepper flakes, and salt and pepper to taste and simmer, stirring occasionally for one more minute.
  6. Turn the heat off and gradually stir in the cheeses in about 3-4 handfuls until completely melted.
