Yummy Carne Asada Recipe

One of the best Carne Asada Recipes you will ever have

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Fresh Cilantro
  • Juice of Two Limes
  • 2 Tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce
  • 5 Cloves Garlic Minced
  • 1 Medium Jalepeno
  • 1 Teaspoon Chilli Powder
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Cumin
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 2 Pounds Flank Steak

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl whisk together the olive oil, cilantro, lime juice, worcestershire sauce, garlic, jalapeno, salt, chili powder, cumin, and pepper to combine.
  2. Add the steak to a large zip top plastic bag, or a long shallow dish and cover with the marinade, turning a few times to coat.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, up to overnight.
  4. Remove the steak from the bag to a large plate and discard the marinade. Allow the steak to sit at room temperature for 30 minutes before grilling.
  5. Preheat your grill to high heat. Cook the steak for 5-8 minutes per side, only turning once until seared and cooked to your desired doneness.
  6. Place the steak on a plate and tent with foil. Allow the steak to rest for 10 minutes. Remove the foil and slice the steak against the grain into thin strips.
  7. Serve immediately with your favorite tortillas and toppings.
