Alfredo Sauce

Yummy Alfredo Sauce Recipe

This is a classic recipe I have been making for years for me and my family

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1 1/2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 Teaspoons Minced Garlic
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Pepper
  • 2 Cups Freshly Grated Parmesean Cheese
